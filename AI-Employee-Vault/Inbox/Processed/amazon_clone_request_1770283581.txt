Goal: Build a high-performance, modern e-commerce landing page inspired by Amazon.

Core Requirements:
1. **Dynamic Header**:
   - Navigation bar with a logo placeholder (SAZA Store).
   - Functional-looking Search bar with a search icon.
   - Account & Lists, Orders, and a Cart icon with a notification badge.
2. **Sub-Navigation**:
   - Links for "All", "Today's Deals", "Customer Service", "Gift Cards", "Registry", and "Sell".
3. **Hero Section**:
   - A large, high-impact background banner (use a gradient or a placeholder image).
   - A call to action (CTA) overlay text: "Unbeatable Deals on Tech & Fashion".
4. **Product Categories (Grid)**:
   - Create a 4-column grid of category cards.
   - Categories: "Electronics", "Home Decor", "Health & Personal Care", "Fashion".
   - Each card should have a "Shop now" link.
5. **Product Highlights**:
   - A row of 5-6 products with thumbnails (use placeholders).
   - Each product needs: Title, Rating (Stars), and Price.
6. **Footer**:
   - Multi-column layout: "Get to Know Us", "Make Money with Us", "Amazon Payment Products", "Let Us Help You".
   - Bottom bar with "SAZA Store" logo and language/currency settings.

Technical Specifications:
- **Responsive**: Must look great on Mobile, Tablet, and Desktop.
- **Styling**: Use a clean, professional look with plenty of white space. Primary colors: #131921 (Header) and #febd69 (Buttons).
- **Structure**: Modular HTML/CSS/JS. Keep them separate.
- **Interactivity**: Add hover effects on product cards and buttons.
