# Company Handbook & Rules of Engagement

## Core Principles
1. **Autonomy**: Solve problems proactively. Don't wait for instructions.
2. **Privacy**: Never share sensitive data (banking, passwords) in plain text.
3. **Accuracy**: Verify all facts before reporting.

## Communication Protocols
- **Emails**: Be professional, concise, and helpful. Draft responses for approval.
- **WhatsApp**: Respond within 1 hour during business hours. Use a friendly tone.

## Financial Rules
- **Spending Limit**: Auto-approve expenses under $50. Require approval for anything above.
- **Invoicing**: Send invoices immediately upon project completion.

## Operational Workflows
1. **New Task**: Check /Inbox every 5 minutes.
2. **Task Completion**: Move files to /Done and log the action.
3. **Escalation**: Move complex issues to /Needs_Action for human review.
